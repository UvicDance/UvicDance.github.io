Executive

The UVic Ballroom Dance Club is run by a group of volunteer students. They hire professional instructors to teach classes, they plan events and workshops. If you are interested in joining the executive and helping run the club, please talk to one of the executive or email us at ballroom@uvicdance.com!

President - Elliott Fuller

This is my third year with the club and the executive and since joining I've had an amazing time. This club has continuously defied my expectations. I had no prior dance experience when I joined, but through the teaching of the amazing instructors, the super friendly (and very patient) members and the great social events that happen every weekend, I've progressed tremendously. The ballroom club is a great way to meet people, and I've made some really good friends this year. Finally, I was completely blown away by the awesome social events that happen around Victoria. Come to Swing City, it will change the way you think about a Friday night. Anyway before I start rambling if you see me at practice or out on the dance floor don't hesitate to approach me with any questions you might have, or just to ask for a dance. I will do my best to assist you in both areas. Happy dancing and see you on the floor!

About Us

  • Welcome to the UVic Ballroom Dance Club (UVBDC)! As a Vikes Rec Club, we believe that ballroom dancing improves physical and social wellbeing and enhances quality of life! We offer lessons not only in Standard Ballroom (like waltz, foxtrot, and tango), but also Latin Ballroom, East Coast Swing, Salsa, Merengue and Bachata. You don’t need a partner or any dance experience (having two left feet is fine), just show up and get ready to have fun, meet new people, and, of course, learn how to dance!.
