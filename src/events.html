The Spring Formal Dance

Join us on Saturday, March 14th, for the UVic Ballroom Dance Club's annual Spring Semester dance! There will be prizes, food and lots of fun dancing! The recommended dress code is semi-formal to formal. No partner or experience required! This is a great opportunity to use what you learned in lessons and come out social dancing! Everyone is welcome, hope to see everyone there :)

When: Saturday, March 14th, 7pm-11pm

Where: Dance Victoria

Tickets:$10 in advance $15 at the door

You can purchase tickets at weekend lessons or message any executives (Alexis Chang, Elliott Fuller, Jill Lambeth, Katrina Annett, Ryan Kim and Nicole Heron) for tickets!
