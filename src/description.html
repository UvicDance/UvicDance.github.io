    Class Descriptions

    The UVic Ballroom Dance Club offers lessons not only in Standard Ballroom (like waltz, foxtrot, and tango), but also Latin Ballroom, East Coast Swing, Salsa, Mergengue and Bachata. As a Vikes rec. club, all classes are taught by professional, paid instructors. You don’t need a partner or any dance experience (having two left feet is fine), just show up, get ready to have fun, meet new people, and, of course, learn how to dance!

    Comfy shoes, clothes and a positive attitude is all you need to bring!

    About Us

    • Welcome to the UVic Ballroom Dance Club (UVBDC)! As a Vikes Rec Club, we believe that ballroom dancing improves physical and social wellbeing and enhances quality of life! We offer lessons not only in Standard Ballroom (like waltz, foxtrot, and tango), but also Latin Ballroom, East Coast Swing, Salsa, Mergengue and Bachata. You don’t need a partner or any dance experience (having two left feet is fine), just show up and get ready to have fun, meet new people, and, of course, learn how to dance!.
