President - Jill Lambeth

I’m excited to say this will be my third year with the UVic Ballroom Club and being part of the Executive team. In my first year at UVic I really wanted to join at least one club so I could meet new people and get involved with the student life. UVic Ballroom turned out to be the perfect choice because it had the social aspect I was looking for, I got to learn a new skill and it was form of exercise! I always look forward to lessons each weekend, but the best part is being able to use the moves I’ve learned at UVic out in the community at dances like Swing City and Nothin’but Swing! I’ve always loved to dance, but now I’m addicted!

Events Coordinator - Ruth Digby

I have been dancing my whole life. When I was 6 I finally started lessons, and haven't stopped since! Growing up, I mainly did ballet, with a few other dances (jazz, contemporary, musical theatre) thrown in for good measure. Now I've found ballroom and it's possibly the most fun I've ever had dancing. It's social, there is so much variety, and everyone in the club has always been incredibly welcoming. Whatever your skill level, come have fun!

These days I can be found at lessons on the weekends, Swing City on Friday nights, and twirling around campus when I think nobody is watching.
