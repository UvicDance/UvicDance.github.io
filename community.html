Where to Dance in Victoria

Dance City

Dance City Dance Night is here! Every Friday, join us for a workshop from 7:30 PM to 8:30 PM, followed by a social dance until 10:30 PM. These events take place at the Victoria Edelweiss Club, 108 Niagara St.

The workshops will feature a different dance styles each week!

Price: $12 for adults, $10 for students and Edelweiss Club Members.

Salsa Caliente Salsa Saturdays

Salsa Caliente's Salsa Saturdays are held on the second Saturday of every month at Studio 4. Each party features a beginner lesson at 8:30 PM to get everyone up and dancing, followed by social dancing to salsa, bachcata, merengue and more until 12 AM

Price: $15 cash at the door or ticks are available on Eventbright.

Red Hot Swing

Red Hot Swing is bringing the energy every Friday night at Dance Victoria, 2750 Quadra Street! Doors open at 7:45 PM, followed by a beginner lesson at 8:00 PM, and social dancing with live music from 9:00 PM to 11:30 PM. You can find more information on their website.

Price: $18 for adults, $15 for students
