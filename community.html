Where to Dance in Victoria




Swing City

  • Run by Denny's Canned Music for over 13 years, Swing City is a social dance including all styles of ballroom! Held on most Friday nights at the Edelweiss German Cultural Center in James Bay, there is always a free one hour dance workshop to kick-off the evening. The dance is on Fridays with the doors opening at 7:45pm and a beginner lesson from 8:00 pm to 9:00 pm. Social dancing is from 9:00 pm to 12:00 am. Cost: $12 for community members or $10 for students.

Cafe Casablanca

  • Cafe Casablanca, located on 2524 Bridge Street, is victoria's only latin dance restaurant. It is the best place to go if you are looking for a night of salsa dancing. Doors open at 8:30pm, and there is a beginner salsa lesson from 8:45-9:30pm. Social dancing is from 9:45pm- 1:00am dancing to Salsa, Bachata, Merengue, Cha Cha Cha and more. All ages welcome. $10.00 per person + $2 min charge at the cafe.

Red Hot Swing

  • The Friday weekly social dance is the place to go to strut your stuff on the dance floor. Total beginners are always welcome; a drop-in beginner lesson will have you dancing swing in no time. Neither partner nor experience are required, but pack a spare sense of humour!Following the lesson, the dance features fantastic jazz music perfect for dancing the night away whether you’re mad about lindy hop, charleston, balboa, or collegiate shag. We’ll have a live band on the last Friday of every month, otherwise you’ll be able dance to the very best DJs’ toe tappin’ tunes. Time: 8:30pm doors open and drop-in beginner lesson starts at 8:35pm, then social dancing from 9:20 until 12:30. Location: 2750 Quadra Street, just north of Hillside in Dance Victoria studios. This beautiful venue is air conditioned! Price (with band): $13 adults, $12 members, $10 for students Price (with DJ): $8 adults, $7 members, $5 for students

Swan's BrewPub

  • Swan's Hotel & Brewpub is the best live band swing dancing you can find on a Monday or Tuesday night. Althought 19+, it is a great place to go with friends, eat, drink and dance! The live band starts at 9pm and the best part is there is no cover. Check out the Swan's website for the band playlists.

About Us

  • Welcome to the UVic Ballroom Dance Club (UVBDC)! As a Vikes Rec Club, we believe that ballroom dancing improves physical and social wellbeing and enhances quality of life! We offer lessons not only in Standard Ballroom (like waltz, foxtrot, and tango), but also Latin Ballroom, East Coast Swing, Salsa, Merengue and Bachata. You don’t need a partner or any dance experience (having two left feet is fine), just show up and get ready to have fun, meet new people, and, of course, learn how to dance!.
