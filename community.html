Where to Dance in Victoria

Swing City

Swing City's workshops and social dances are back! Every Friday they have a workshop from 8pm until 9pm, followed by a social dance until 11pm. They take place at the Victoria Edelweiss Club, 108 Niagra St.
The workshops will feature a variety of different dance styles every week!

Price: $12 for adults, $10 for students and Edelweiss Club Members

Salsa Caliente

Salsa Caliente is currently located at 727 Yates St, Victoria. Salsa classes are taught Mondays and Tuesdays, while bachata and merengue are on Thursdays. Social dances are held every Thursday from 9pm to 10pm.

Price: $15 per class (paid monthly), $5 for a social dance.

SALSA CALIENTE SALSA SATURDAYS
Salsa Caliente's Salsa Saturdays are held on the second saturday of every month at Sunset Labs.
Price: $15 cash at the door.

Red Hot Swing

Starting March 18th, Red Hot Swing will be running social dances every Monday and Friday at 1110 Hillside Ave. The Friday dances will begin with a beginner lesson. You can find more information on their website.

Price (with band): $18 for adults, $15 for students
Price (with DJ): $13 for adults, $10 for students
